Facts & Features

Located in Roaring Branch PA, this property offers stunning views of Laurel Ridge! Here is the opportunity to create your family retreat! This quiet, secluded farm is on a gated, private road at the top of a mountain. The structure is a typical Pennsylvania farm house built in the early 1900s. In need of some TLC, it is being sold “as is”. The tillable acres are leased annually to a local family farm.
